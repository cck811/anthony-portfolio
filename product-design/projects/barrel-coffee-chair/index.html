50-Gallon Barrel Coffee Chair

A sustainable furniture concept using industrial waste

Overview

This compact project explores sustainability in furniture design by repurposing a 50-gallon industrial barrel into a functional, minimalist coffee chair. The project demonstrates quick ideation and efficient material use while reducing waste and embracing the aesthetics of reclaimed materials.

Design Goals

  • Transform an industrial barrel into a usable, ergonomic piece of furniture
  • Embrace sustainable design by minimizing waste and avoiding virgin materials
  • Develop a concept that can be fabricated with basic tools and standard CAD planning

Concept Sketch

The initial sketch explores the form factor and cut lines for turning a standard barrel into a low-profile coffee chair, focusing on comfort and structural simplicity.

CAD Modeling

Using Rhino, I created a simplified CAD model to visualize the final proportions and simulate the cuts and placement of structural reinforcements. This model served as the basis for potential fabrication planning.

Reflection
