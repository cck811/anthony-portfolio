Objective:

Be the first player to collect 12 points from the cards. But here's the catch, some cards might set you back!

How to Play:

  • Player 1 will start the game.

  • Each turn, click on one of the cards to score. 4 of the cards will add points to your score and 2 of the cards take points away.

  • The 'Test My Luck' button is available at the start of each turn. This gives you a chance to pick two cards instead of one. But use this option wisely, as one of the card might reset your scores.

  • After picking a card (or two, if you've tested your luck), the turn automatically switches to the other player.

Winning the Game:

The first player to reach or exceed 12 points wins the game!
