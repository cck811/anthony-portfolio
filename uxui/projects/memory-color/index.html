Bringing Memory Color to Life – Design, Marketing & Rebrand

Project Overview

Memory Color 3D Print Studio offers custom 3D printed figurines and photo-based relief artwork. I was brought in as a designer and marketing specialist to assist with the rebranding and customer engagement strategies as the company scaled its services.

My Role

My responsibilities at Memory Color included:

  • Refreshing the brand identity, logo, and design system
  • Designing and managing social media content and advertising campaigns
  • Optimizing user flows for the online service process (guide → upload → selection → order)
  • Product photography and marketing visuals
  • Creating product renders and promotional graphics
  • Providing bilingual (English/Chinese) customer support and internal communication assistance
  • Collaborating with the core team on design-focused product improvements

Tools and Technologies Used Mainly

  • Design: Illustrator, Photoshop, Lightroom, After Effects, Canva
  • Developement: Figma, Weebly, Jotform
  • Marketing: Capcut, Filmora, Instagram Reels, social media scheduling tools
  • Communication: Bilingual client service and internal coordination

Rebranding & Design System

As part of the rebranding, I developed a refreshed visual identity for Memory Color, including an updated design system that communicates warmth, creativity, and trust. This design system was applied consistently across digital platforms, marketing materials, and newly designed packaging for the studio's products.

Marketing & Customer Engagement

I supported marketing initiatives by directing and filming promotional content for Instagram. Our most successful reels featured transformation videos, studio behind-the-scenes, and emotional customer testimonials.

  • Assisted with scalable content strategy for Instagram reels
  • Produced, edited, and published original video/photo content
  • Designed story highlight icons, profile banners, and booking instructions
See marketing page here

Challenges and Solutions

  • Challenge: Communicating product value to first-time customers
    • Solution: Developed visual assets that clearly explained the process and results
  • Challenge: Improving consistency in branding and marketing
    • Solution: Created a reusable content system and updated brand visuals
  • Challenge: Supporting cross-cultural communication within the team
    • Solution: Helped facilitate bilingual communication and smooth work flow

Outcomes & Reflections

This project gave me the opportunity to apply my UX and design skills in a live business setting. I contributed to a rebrand that enhanced visual cohesion and user clarity, and helped expand marketing efforts that increased customer engagement and visibility.
